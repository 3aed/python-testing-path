# Setting a Foundation for Successful Test Automation

## Summary

Many companies begin their test automation journey with **proof of concepts**, rather than a proper **strategy**. This often leads to failed test automation initiatives that do not meet expectations.  

In this course, we will explore the initial considerations for starting a new test automation initiative, including:

---

### Key Learning Points

1. **Developing a Test Automation Strategy**  
   Learn how to create a strategy that aligns with your business needs.

2. **Cultivating a Supportive Culture**  
   Build an organizational culture conducive to the success of your test automation initiative.

3. **Developer Techniques**  
   Techniques that application developers can employ to support test automation.

4. **Choosing the Right Tools**  
   How to select tools that fit your test automation goals and requirements.

5. **Early Considerations**  
   Prepare your project for future growth by considering key factors early on.

6. **Optimizing and Scaling Automation**  
   Strategies to optimize and scale your automation project according to business needs.

7. **Quantifying ROI**  
   Measure the return on investment and communicate the value of test automation within your organization.

---

> **Tip:** The success of test automation depends not just on tools and scripts, but on strategy, culture, and alignment with business objectives.
